Banku with Tilapia

Banku with Grilled Tilapia

The famous banku and Tilapia have taken over the Ghanaian nightlife

Some people call the famous banku and tilapia dish a portion of food for the champions or warriors probably because it's an energy-packed cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Corn dough
  • Cassava dough
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Fresh Tilapia
  • Natural spices such as garlic, ginger, rosemary
  • Pepper
  • Onion
