Jollof Recipe

Ghana Jollof Rice

This food is a celebrated dish in Ghana. It's a loved by the young and old

One will need rice, tomoatoes oil pepper, etc

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomoatoes
  • Oil
  • Smoked Fish
  • Pepper
  • Spices
  • Chicken

Steps

  1. Get Heating source
  2. Get an empty saucepan
  3. Set it on fire
  4. Pour a reasonable amount of oil into the pan
  5. Add your Ingredients
  6. Leave to steam
  7. Add the smoked for flavour
  8. Leave to steam for some minutes
  9. Add the rice
  10. Stir to get a uniform mixture
  11. Leaave to steam for about 3 minutes
  12. Stir and add reasonable amount of water
  13. Leave to boil and stir as need be.
  14. Food is ready Serve Hot
