Mashed Kenkey Recipe

This is a special drink enjoyed by most Ghanaian especially the Fnate community

This food is made from a local cooked corn dough which is conciled in a plantain leave

Ingredients

  • A ball or two of Kenkey
  • A blender or Man power approach
  • Water be it cold or normal room temperature
  • Groundnut
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Ice Cubes

Steps

  1. Get your Kenkey
  2. Unwrap the kenkey from the plantain leaves
  3. Wash it under running water
  4. chop it into smaller pieces
  5. Put the chopped pieces into the blender
  6. Add reasonable amount of water
  7. Power the blender
  8. Add the Groundnut milk and sugar
  9. Serve in glass
